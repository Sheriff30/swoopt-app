VÄLKOMMEN!
In the next 5 minutes, you will learn everything you need to become a guru with Swoopt!
DOWNLOAD THE SWOOPT APP
  1. Open the App Store on your mobile phone
    Android
    iPhone
  2. Search for the "Swoopt" App
  3. Download the Swoopt App to your mobile phone
REGISTER IN THE SWOOPT APP

  1. On your mobile phone, launch the Swoopt App

    Swoopt
  2. Select "UPPSALA" as your city
  3. Go to "Settings" (in the top-right corner) and tap on "My Profile"
  4. Complete your profile details (be sure to use the same mobile number that you provided to the Swoopt team)

Note: For security reasons, you will need to enter a one-time pass (OTP) which will automatically be texted to you

CHECK "MY BUSINESS PROFILE"
  1. In the top-right corner, tap on "Settings"
  2. Confirm that all of your businesses are displaying

OBS! If one or more of your businesses is not displaying, email us at info@swoopt.app

CREATE YOUR FIRST OFFER(S)
  1. In Settings, tap on "MY MARKETING"
  2. Tap on "+ CREATE NEW"
  3. Fill in the required fields (see tips below):
    • Heading: should exactly match the flyer design (attached in email)
    • Sub-heading: should exactly match the flyer design (attached in email)
    • Villkor: Type in any relevant condiitions (eg. one per customer)
    • Inlösningar: how many times can a customer redeem this offer?
    • Est. Savings: how much do they save for each redemption?
    • Startdatum: this should be the launch date (dd/mm/yyyy)
    • Utgångsdatum: we suggest dd/mm/yyyy (4 weeks duration)
    • Streckkod: optional
  4. When ready, tap "Publicera" to finish.

    5. Note: your offer will be reviewed by the Swoopt team. Once approved, your offer will go live on the selected start date.

  5. If you have more than one launch offer, follow the steps above for each offer
MY PREVIOUS MARKETING (EXPLAINED)

At any time, you can check how well your Swoopt marketing is working

  1. In Settings, tap on "MY MARKETING"

  2. Tap on "MY PREVIOUS MARKETING"

    Here you will see how many times each offer has been redeemed.

  3. Fill in the required fields (see tips below):

      STATUSES EXPLAINED:

      Active = any offer that is live (or scheduled to go live).

      Pending = any offer that has been submitted and under review by the Swoopt team.

      Rejected* = any offer that has been submitted, but was rejected by the Swoopt team.

      Expired = any offer that has now expired.

      * Offers can be rejected for a number of reasons (including spelling mistakes, errors, legal or quality reasons).

  4. OBS!

    5. If you ever wish to re-launch a previously successful offer, you can simply tap on that offer and select "RE-LAUNCH".

    If you ever wish to cancel an offer before it's scheduled expiry date, simply tap on that offer and select "EXPIRE".

MY STATISTICS (EXPLAINED)
  1. In Settings, tap on "MY STATISTICS"
  2. These statistics give you real-time results on how effective Swoopt is for your business.
  3. Note: the "Survey Resuts" button is only active if you have chosen to activate the Swoopt Survey feature. If this feature is not currently active, simply drop us an email at info@swoopt.app to get it activated.
MY SAVINGS (EXPLAINED)
  1. In Settings, tap on "MY SAVINGS"
  2. Note, this is a customer feature (showing shoppers how much they have saved by redeeming Swoopt offers).
  3. If you choose to redeem coupons at other businesses, then these savings will also be relevant to you as a shopper.
